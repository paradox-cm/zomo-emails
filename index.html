Template Standards

All ZOMO Health email templates follow consistent design and technical standards:

  • Brand Consistency: Unified color palette, typography, and visual elements
  • Mobile Responsive: Optimized for all device sizes and email clients
  • Accessibility: WCAG compliant with proper contrast and semantic markup
  • Email Client Support: Tested across Outlook, Gmail, Apple Mail, and major clients
  • HIPAA Compliance: Healthcare-specific security and privacy considerations
  • Performance Optimized: Fast loading with efficient HTML structure

Implementation Guide

Getting started with ZOMO Health email templates:

  1. Choose Template: Select the appropriate template for your email type
  2. Customize Content: Replace placeholder text with your specific content
  3. Update Branding: Ensure logos, colors, and contact information are current
  4. Test Responsiveness: Preview on desktop and mobile devices
  5. Email Client Testing: Verify rendering across major email clients
  6. Deploy: Import into your email marketing platform or send directly

Best Practices

  • Content Strategy: Keep messages clear, concise, and actionable
  • Personalization: Use dynamic content to improve engagement
  • Call-to-Actions: Make CTAs prominent and action-oriented
  • Image Optimization: Use compressed images with proper alt text
  • Link Management: Use tracking URLs and ensure all links work
  • Compliance: Include required unsubscribe and preference management links
  • Testing: Always test before sending to your full audience
